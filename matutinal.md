1. matutinal, matinal (in entomological writings); terms used in the life sciences to indicate something of, relating to, or occurring in the early morning^[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matutinal]
	1. The term may describe crepuscular animals that are significantly active during the predawn or early morning hours. During the morning twilight period and shortly thereafter, these animals partake in important tasks, such as scanning for mates, mating, and foraging.
	2. matutine
