1. bliss point ([[food]]); the amount of an ingredient such as [[salt]], [[sugar]] or [[fat]] which optimizes deliciousness
   
   Combinations of sugar, fat, and salt act synergistically, and are more rewarding than any one alone. In food product optimization, the goal is to include two or three of these nutrients at their bliss point.^[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bliss_point_(food)]
